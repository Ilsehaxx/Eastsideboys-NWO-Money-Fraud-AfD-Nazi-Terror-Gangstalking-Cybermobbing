# Eastsideboyz Research Summary

## Overview
Eastsideboyz is a German rap collective focused on Eastern German (Ossi) culture, drug use themes, partying, and anti-establishment narratives. Founded by ArniTheSavage from Halle (Saale), they produce music blending German rap with techno/hardtekk influences. No evidence of GRU influence found; appears to be independent artists with potential concerns about youth radicalization through drug glorification.
